Our Mission

YFC (Youth For Christ) Sligo is an organisation apart of YFC International - a worldwide organisation dedicated to sharing the good news that Jesus christ has come to seek and save the lost.

YFC Sligo is a small group that meets each month to share in worship and praise, the groups also travel to different events around Ireland to make an impact on the youth of today.

Our Team

Our team is a small but passionate group of individuals who are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of young people. Each member brings their unique talents and energy,

creating a warm and welcoming environment for everyone who enters our doors. Each member of the team is a volunteer and gives up their time to help witness as a follower of Jesus Christ.

Outreach Programs

We believe in reaching out to those who need support and encouragement. Through these programs, we share hope, joy,

and the message of love in a way that resonates with everyone. Some of the programs we run are: fundrasing events, youth day outings, and worship nights.

Community Events

Bringing people together is at the heart of what we do. Our community events are filled with laughter, music, and meaningful conversations.

Whether it's a youth night, a worship meeting, or a fun day out, we ensure that everyone feels included and valued.

Future Goals

Looking ahead, we are excited to expand our reach and impact. Our goal is to create more opportunities for young people to grow, learn, and thrive. With the support of our community,

we are confident that we can continue to make a positive difference in the lives of many. And serve the king of kings with all our hearts.
